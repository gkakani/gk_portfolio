About me

I am currently pursuing Masters in Health Informatics at Indiana University, Indianapolis, driven by my passion for the intersection of health and IT. My journey into the world of data began in healthcare, where I started my career as a Junior Data Analyst at Cognizant in India. This experience sparked my curiosity and desire to delve deeper into data analytics, prompting my pursuit of a Master's degree. Through my coursework, I've built a solid foundation in data analysis with various technical tools and am eager to apply this knowledge in real-world settings across both the health industry and other sectors. Working with a global team and gaining a blend of technical, business, and analytical skills is my goal. As a continuous learner, I am always ready to embrace new challenges and opportunities to transform data into meaningful insights that drive business success.
